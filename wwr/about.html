Our History

As a company that started over 20 years ago, we have a solid background of rafting experience, starting primarily as a family business and evolving to a company with one purpose: to share that experience with others and ensure individuals and families get the best of Deepwater Rafting Services. We believe in making bonds with people, and traveling through the rapids and rivers creates the perfect environment to deepen those bonds. "Adventure Runs Deep in our Blood" is the motto we share with everyone who joins us.
