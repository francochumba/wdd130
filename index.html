Sixth Region

The Sixth Region, located near the center of Chile, is known for its history, culture, and agricultural economy, especially raisins and fruit crops. It is home to "El Teniente", the world's largest open-pit copper mine, and several natural parks such as Río Los Cipreses and Las Palmas de Cocalán.
